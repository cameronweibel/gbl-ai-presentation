Adoption

Mandates Don't Drive Adoption. Discovery Does.

The most effective enterprise AI rollouts follow a curated discovery pattern.

  • Curated performance discovery period — let people find tools that genuinely help their specific workflow
  • Approval gates, not mandates — define what's needed for compliance, then step back
  • Come back in a few weeks — collect the tools people actually adopted, not the ones you prescribed
  • Surface organic adoption to management — show what people chose to use and why
  • Tier by seniority — different tools and agents are relevant at each rung in the hierarchy

AI digester agents can be personalised per partner. Aggregated and summarised prospectuses delivered at the frequency and depth each person actually wants.

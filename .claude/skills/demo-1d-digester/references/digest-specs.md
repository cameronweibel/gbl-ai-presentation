# Digest Specifications by Level

## Analyst (800-1200 words)
**Audience:** Junior team members doing modeling and memo work.
- All findings with full quotes
- Raw data points: growth rates, margins, guidance numbers
- Management tone indicators with specific language examples
- Risk assessment with trend direction
- Comparable quarter references
- Action items: what to update in model, what to flag for senior review

## Associate (400-600 words)
**Audience:** Mid-level team building the investment narrative.
- Key themes synthesized across all prompts (not prompt-by-prompt)
- Risk flags with severity ranking
- Comparison signals vs prior quarter
- Signal summary table
- 2-3 most important quotes only
- Suggested follow-up questions for management

## VP (200-300 words)
**Audience:** Senior team making portfolio-level decisions.
- Executive summary (3-4 sentences)
- Thesis alignment score + one-line rationale
- Only material changes from prior quarters
- Hold/increase/decrease/exit signal
- One key risk to watch

## Partner (50-100 words)
**Audience:** Partners who need "should I care about this?"
- One paragraph verdict: what happened, does it matter, what to do
- Binary signal: ATTENTION NEEDED or NO ACTION REQUIRED
- If nothing changed: "No material change. Thesis intact."
